**Definition**  
For variables X and Y,
$\rho_{X,Y} \;=\; \frac{\operatorname{cov}(X,Y)}{\sigma_X \sigma_Y}$
In sample form (most common in practice):
$r \;=\; \frac{\sum_i (x_i-\bar x)(y_i-\bar y)}{\sqrt{\sum_i (x_i-\bar x)^2}\sqrt{\sum_i (y_i-\bar y)^2}}$
